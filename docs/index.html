Welcome

This is the living rules document for Cazadores, a cooperative miniatures game of daring hunts.

Written by Jose ‘Jae’ Espinoza. A product of Matachin Games.

Get Model sheets, play aids, counters, and support the game at Cazadores on Itch.io

Follow development on the blog at Matachin Games.

Any feedback is welcome to me (Jae) at matachingames@pm.me

Inspiration

This game is built upon the innovations, thrills, and genius of a host of other games and pieces of media. To the best of the author’s abilities, connections between this game and these various works are drawn in Appendix N.

Please seek out and explore these other works.

I hope you enjoy them as much as I do.
